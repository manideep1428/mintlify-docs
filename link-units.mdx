title: 'Link to units'
descriptions: 'This section explains how to create direct links to specific units inside your plan viewwer'
---

This feature is extremely useful for marketing purposes. For example, you can create an email campaign for a specific set of units (such as penthouses) and directly link to those specific units inside your plan viewer, inside your website!

The basic parameter structure for your URL, as shown in the video below, is like so: `www.project.com/plans?f=floorname&u=unitname` where `floorname` and `unitname` are your own values.
