---
title: "360° project"
description: "This new feature allows you to create a 360º outlook of your project’s environment and enhance your user‘s experience."
---

<Note />

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Access the configuration module within your project dashboard and navigate to the **Exterior** tab.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/1zLnW28F/360-1.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2">
    Arrange the desired views in the preferred sequence for presentation within your project. Simply click on the **plus** button to incorporate various views into the display order.

    <Tip>**Important tip:** upload your images in the desired order for display</Tip>

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/26hqgGWK/360-2.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3">
    After organizing the images, you need to draw the simple polygons on each floor of each render. Just click on **Draw** or **Redraw** and repeat this process until finished.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/MHwcwBbK/360-3.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
