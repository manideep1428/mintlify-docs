There are many things you can modify in your projects. Below, you will find instructions on how to make these changes.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Update the project details">
    Go to settings, and under **project details**, you can modify the main image, name and namespace of the project. 
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/X7PTJMV8/change-info-1.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Refine your customization">
    As easy as it looks in the following video. Just select your desired modifications based on the project phase.
<iframe src="https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/oto4h5vgjh?seo=false&amp;videoFoam=true" title="Allocations Video" allow="autoplay; fullscreen" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" class="wistia_embed" name="wistia_embed" width="100%" height="400px"></iframe>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Modify the exterior drawing">
    After updating the main render, you will need to modify the exterior drawing. Simply navigate to the **configuration** module, click on the **exterior** tab, and _redraw_ the corresponding floors.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/DfXp7xjV/update-exterior-ima.jpg" />
    </Frame>
    <Frame caption="Redraw the floors you would like to change. Don't forget to save your work.">
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/d1kFsGXW/update-exterior-image-2.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Update your floor plans">
    Navigate to the **Floors** tab and select **Floorplan** to change the actual floor plate. Then, click on **Draw Floorplan** for each unit and sketch the corresponding polygon.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/TYMpLPT7/update-exterior-drawing-3.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Update units">
    In the **Units** tab, you can update your unit list either _manually_ (one by one) or _automatically_ by re-uploading an updated version of the CSV file.
    <Frame caption="If you have any issues updating your unit list, please contact us.">
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/13k86chq/update-units.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Modify the project's style and embed">
    In this tab, you can freely modify your embedding and hosting options, style colors, and the project's layout.
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/wTsCbhR2/update-style-and-embed.jpg" />
    </Frame>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
